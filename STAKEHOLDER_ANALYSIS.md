# ServeIQ — Stakeholder Analysis

> **Version:** 1.0  
> **Last Updated:** June 2026

---

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Business Owner

**Who:** The person who owns or manages the hospitality business (bar, lounge, club, restaurant).

**Pain Points:**
- No real-time visibility into sales
- Suspects revenue leakage but cannot prove it
- Cannot easily see which waiter performed best
- Relies on manual cash counts at end of night
- Billing errors reduce profitability silently

**Goals:**
- Know the exact revenue figure at any moment
- Catch and prevent theft or underreporting
- Reduce operational stress on waiters
- Professional receipts that build customer trust

**How ServeIQ Helps:**
- Real-time dashboard with live revenue
- Waiter-level sales accountability
- Audit trail for every order and payment
- Automated, accurate receipts

**Decision Maker:** Yes — they sign up and pay for the subscription.

---

### 2. Waiter

**Who:** The floor staff who serve customers, take orders, and deliver bills.

**Pain Points:**
- Manual calculation errors lead to salary deductions
- Cannot track multiple tables simultaneously without stress
- Disputes with customers due to lack of digital records
- Embarrassing moments when bill is wrong in front of customers

**Goals:**
- Finish shift without a billing error
- Be protected by a transparent record
- Work faster and focus on service rather than arithmetic

**How ServeIQ Helps:**
- All calculations automated
- Digital record of every order — no more "he said / she said"
- Simple, fast interface under 10 seconds per action

**Decision Maker:** No — but adoption depends on them. If they resist, the product fails. UI/UX must win them over.

---

### 3. Branch Manager

**Who:** Oversees daily operations of one branch. May also be the owner in small businesses.

**Pain Points:**
- Cannot respond to problems in real time
- No easy shift summary at end of night
- Disputes require manual review of paper records
- Cannot fairly evaluate waiter performance

**Goals:**
- Know what's happening on the floor without walking around constantly
- Produce reliable end-of-day reports quickly
- Handle billing disputes with evidence
- Identify top-performing waiters

**How ServeIQ Helps:**
- Live tab monitoring dashboard
- Waiter performance data
- Digital audit trail for dispute resolution
- Daily sales reports

---

### 4. Customers (Indirect Stakeholder)

**Who:** People eating and drinking at the establishment.

**Pain Points:**
- Billing disputes with waiters
- Long waits while waiter calculates the bill
- No receipt or proof of what they paid

**Goals:**
- Accurate, fast bills
- Clear receipt

**How ServeIQ Helps (Indirectly):**
- Bill calculated instantly — no wait
- Itemized receipt available immediately
- Customers can verify every item before paying

---

## Secondary Stakeholders

### 5. Cashier (V2)

Handles final payment confirmation. Will use ServeIQ to:
- View bills awaiting payment
- Record payment method and reference
- Close tabs and generate receipts

### 6. Inventory Officer (V2)

Manages stock and procurement. Will use ServeIQ to:
- Record stock purchases
- Monitor stock levels
- View variance reports

### 7. ServeIQ Platform Team (Internal)

Manages the platform, subscriptions, and business support.
- Access to Super Admin dashboard
- Can view all business data for support purposes
- Cannot modify business financial records

## Stakeholder Adoption Risk

| Stakeholder | Adoption Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Business Owner | Low — they see immediate value | Clear onboarding, ROI messaging |
| Waiter | High — change resistance, tech unfamiliarity | Ultra-simple UI, max 15-min training, positions system as their protector |
| Branch Manager | Low — gains visibility they've always wanted | Feature-led demo of live dashboard |
| Customer | None — they don't directly use the app | N/A |
